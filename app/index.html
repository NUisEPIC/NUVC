Who can apply?

All undergraduate, graduate and postdoc students (including Feinberg residents) currently attending Northwestern University are eligible to apply. At least one member of every team must currently study at Northwestern University in one of the undergraduate- or graduate-level schools at the time of the competition. Each team must have at least one Northwestern student on the management team of the business (i.e. teams only meet the Northwestern affiliation requirement if at least one of the said Northwestern student(s) are represented on the management team).
